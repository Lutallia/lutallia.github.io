Geometry Dash!

Brief Introduction

Geometry Dash (abbr. GD or GmD) is a 2D runner-style game developped by RobTop Games, whose founder is a Swedish game developer named Robert Topala. Until now it has a history for about 8 years. Many People consider GmD as a relaxing mini game, which was indeed the case at the beginning, but after a couple of years of rapid development, especially thank to the contribution from the players, it has grown into a marvelous, amazing subculture in the domain of gaming.


This fact, however, is not realized by a lot of beginners of this game. That's the purpose of this small web-based brochure - I hope it can guide those who just started playing GmD but know little about it into the big, real world we created based on this game.
